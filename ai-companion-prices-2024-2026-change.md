## Why Prices Increased

Across all platforms, pricing changes were driven by:

- higher GPU compute cost
- more complex multimodal models
- global inflation
- demand for video and voice
- memory and personalization tech

These features require constant servers, which cost money even when users aren't chatting.

## What to Expect Next

Through 2026, you can expect:

- more aggressive yearly discounts
- fewer "unlimited" monthly plans
- more token systems
- occasional price increases on monthly renewals
